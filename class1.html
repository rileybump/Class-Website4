Developing Intelligent Content

ENGL 4814

Carlos Evia

Dr. Evia got his PhD from Texas Tech and researches in Technical and Professional Communication, User Assistance, Structured Authoring, Content Strategy, and Workplace Safety Communication. Aside from being a professor, Dr. Evia is the Associate Dean for Transdisciplinary Initiatives and Chief Technology Officer at Virginia Tech.

Course Summary

This course is an introduction to developing content in the ways that it is consumed in the modern world. This can be social media posts, websites, or presentations. This class will provide the skills to use content management systems to make content that translates from websites to apps to textbooks as efficiently as possible, to make the students in the class high-level, high-level, employees once they graduate.
