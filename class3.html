Intro to Critical Reading

ENGL 2604

Shoshana Knapp

Dr. Knapp got her PhD at Stanford and researches Victorian/Edwardian novelists, 19th-century Russian fiction, Ayn Rand, classical narrative cinema, and 19th-century French fiction.

Course Summary

This course is an introduction to critical reading skills and the writing and revising process. It is a writing-intensive course designed to make the students better readers and writers with the basic skills that future classes will build on in more specific ways.
