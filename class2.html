Intro to Professional and Technical Writing

ENGL2844

Jim Dubinsky

Dr. Dubinsky got his PhD from Miami University and he does research in the Scholarship of Teaching, the Scholarship of Engagement, Professional and Technical Communication, and with Veterans in Society. Dr. Dubinksy is a professor as well as the founding director English Department's Professional Writing program, and helped create the first liberal arts PhD at Virginia Tech.

Course Summary

This course is designed to provide an introduction to professional writing, including giving interviews, writing letters, memos, presentations, and reports. This class is designed to lay the groundwork for more advanced classes in future semesters, but is also for non-English majors by providing basic skills for writing in professional settings.
