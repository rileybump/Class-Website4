British Literary History

ENGL 2544

Andrew Wadoski

Dr. Wadoski received his PhD from the University of Rochester and researches Spenser, Milton, premodern poetics, the history of moral philosophy, and the history of capitalism. He is an associate editor of Milton Quarterly and has served on the executive committee of the International Spenser Society.

Course Summary

This course covers British literature from the seventh century to the present. It focuses on literary history and how literature looks at the changing cultural contexts of Britain over more than a thousand years, from the Roman Empire to the modern world.
